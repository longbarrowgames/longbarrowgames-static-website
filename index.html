The Studio

Long Barrow Games is a solo indie studio. We're currently working on our first release title, Damn Quest, a challenging flight focused exploration game.

Damn Quest

A young Pukeko, lost within a vast forest spread over a long-forgotten continent. Master the air with our fun but challenging flying mechanics and gather grapes to reveal the buried relics and strengthening runes scattered throughout the world. Search the ruins you encounter along the way, as legends tell of hidden treasures in long lost places.

Damn Quest has been designed to make modding a streamlined, drag-and-drop process for both environmental objects and cosmetic items. Full modding tutorials will be available on YouTube before launch.

Developer

I grew up in a village close to Stonehenge and very little else, so spent most of my free time exploring the woods and coppices of the surrounding countryside. The Barrows, and what could be buried beneath them, always caught my imagination more than the Stones themselves.
I hope to create experiences that capture the sense of freedom and adventure I felt when exploring that prehistoric landscape.
